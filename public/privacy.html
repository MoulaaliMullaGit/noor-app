Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Noor ("Read. Reflect. Return.") is a mobile application that helps users build a daily Quran reading habit. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data we collect

Noor does not collect, store, or process any personal data on its own servers. All user data — including bookmarks, reading streaks, goals, reflections, and preferences — is stored and managed by Quran Foundation through their APIs. When you sign in, you authenticate directly with Quran Foundation's OAuth2 system.

Authentication

Noor uses Quran Foundation's OAuth2 with PKCE flow for authentication. Your login credentials are never seen or stored by Noor. Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using encrypted storage provided by the operating system.

Third-party services

Noor integrates with Quran Foundation APIs (quran.foundation) to provide Quranic content, audio, translations, tafsir, and user features. Please refer to Quran Foundation's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle your data.

Data stored on your device

Noor stores authentication tokens and your app preferences (such as selected translation language, reciter, and font size) locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server other than Quran Foundation's APIs for authentication purposes.

Analytics and tracking

Noor does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not track your behavior, location, or usage patterns.

Children's privacy

Noor does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app's content is suitable for all ages.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at noorapp.contact@gmail.com.
